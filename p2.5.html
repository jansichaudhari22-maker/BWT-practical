About IT

IT revolves around the acquisition, storage, processing, and transmission of data. The primary goal of IT is to improve efficiency, productivity, and communication within organizations and among individuals. However, the scope of IT extends beyond mere hardware and software.








Cyber Security

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting digital devices, networks, and sensitive data from cyber threats such as hacking, malware, and phishing attacks." It involves a range of strategies, technologies, and best practices designed to safeguard computers, networks, and data from cyber attacks.
